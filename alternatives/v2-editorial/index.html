Epistack

How Many Voices Is This Really?

Finding the claim a contested conclusion rests on—and testing whether its support is as independent as it looks.

Epistack is a human–AI research workflow for contested or technically layered questions. It builds on a hybrid RAG system and preserves the working state beneath a research synthesis: separate source-cited AI judgments, a proposed load-bearing research claim, and typed documentary relationships—shared authors, data, advisers, methods—each tied to its exact supporting passage.

A fluent narrative can hide which reviewer weighted which evidence, whether several citations represent separate support or one related body of work, and which observation would most change the picture. The output is a versioned case record another investigator can challenge, extend, or correct without recreating the whole inquiry.

Two questions

  1. What specific claim is carrying a contested conclusion?

  2. How independent is the support behind that claim?

Epistack · Problem and Approach Slide 02 / 07

A polished synthesis can hide the state of the inquiry.

What disappears inside one narrative

  • The load-bearing claimWhich specific claim carries the conclusion?
  • The independence of supportDo several sources share authors, data, advice, methods, or upstream analyses?
  • The change conditionWhat evidence would alter the current judgment?

What Epistack preserves

  1. Build a traceable corpus and record exclusions.
  2. Keep source-cited judgments separate before synthesis.
  3. Compare those judgments to propose a concrete Crux.
  4. Record source relationships only with supporting passages.
  5. Package evidence, objections, missing evidence, and revisions as a case.

How much of the observed concentration of early known cases around the Huanan market survives correction for the way those cases were found?

Market-focused judgmentModerate weight as evidence of an early center, but not conclusive.

Mangano coauthored both the official report and the principal fallback paper. The fallback paper acknowledges the report’s other four authors among colleagues who supplied guidance and advice.

2 → 12passages retained per reviewer in a deeper retrieval run. Both bylines appeared; the panel judgments did not connect them, and the adviser acknowledgment was not retrieved.

These links do not establish shared data, invalid reasoning, adviser endorsement, or danger. They show why the case record must preserve typed, passage-supported relationships instead of treating citation count as independence.

Epistack · Perplexity Deep Research Comparison Slide 06 / 07

The comparison is about the research artifact.

One instructed Deep Research run received the same COVID question and ten-source starting set under a protocol fixed before the call.

Deep Research

Produced the better single narrative.

  • It returned a concise 258-word synthesis.
  • When directly asked, it named the tension between transmission signal and ascertainment bias.
  • It found useful shared-authorship and debate relationships.
Epistack

Preserves a different record.

  • Separate judgments, cited passages, and the proposed Crux remain inspectable.
  • Relationship types and corrections remain attached to source evidence.
  • The comparison audit corrected an unsupported expansion from shared authorship to a shared-dataset claim.

Limit: one instructed run does not establish typical product behavior or an overall Epistack advantage.
