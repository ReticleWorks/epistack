# Audited comparison

## What the run did well

The single Deep Research run produced the stronger integrated narrative. After the prompt directly requested it, the result named the practical hinge: whether the observed geographic pattern reflects transmission or ascertainment bias. It also noticed real source relationships, including shared authorship and debate ancestry. Epistack cannot claim that either capability is unique.

## What the returned record lacked

The result was a 258-word answer plus fifteen URLs, not the requested full report and source-use table. It did not attach exact supporting passages to its claims, did not clearly identify a decision-changing observation, and did not provide a method for its 65–80% confidence range. The returned structure also did not identify which search results supported which sentence.

## Relationship claim checked against the fixed sources

The result said sources 1–3 "share authors and a single case dataset."

**Supported:** the three papers share authors. The source record lists Andersen, Rambaut, Holmes, and Garry on sources 1 and 3, and those four among the authors of source 2.

**Not established:** one case dataset across all three papers. The fixed sources centrally use different evidence:

- source 1 analyzes December case locations, environmental samples, wildlife-sale evidence, and related population and mobility data;
- source 2 analyzes early viral genomes, phylogenetic rooting, phylodynamic models, and epidemic simulations; and
- source 3 analyzes viral genomic features and predates the two 2022 market papers.

The public source record therefore labels this relationship **shared authorship**, not **one shared case dataset**. That correction does not show that the papers are wrong. It prevents one relationship type from silently becoming another.

## Comparison boundary

This is one instructed run. It does not establish typical Perplexity behavior or overall Epistack superiority. Deep Research produced a useful answer and found the hinge. Epistack produces a different artifact: separate judgments, a proposed claim, typed relationship records, exact supporting locations, and corrections remain available for inspection.

Whether that artifact helps readers reason better requires a comparative user study that has not been run.
