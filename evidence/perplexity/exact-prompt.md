# Exact prompt supplied for the Perplexity Deep Research call

Conduct a deep research report on this question:

**Does the spatial clustering of early COVID-19 cases around the Huanan Seafood Market represent the outbreak's true origin, or downstream amplification of prior human-to-human transmission?**

Use the following ten sources as a fixed starting corpus. Examine all that you can access. You may add sources needed to check, challenge, or update this record, but clearly identify which sources you added. Distinguish peer-reviewed research from preprints, commentary, debate material, and advocacy.

1. https://doi.org/10.1126/science.abp8715
2. https://doi.org/10.1126/science.abp8337
3. https://doi.org/10.1038/s41591-020-0820-9
4. https://doi.org/10.1016/j.cell.2021.08.017
5. https://doi.org/10.1093/jrsssa/qnae021
6. https://www.rootclaim.com/analysis/What-is-the-source-of-COVID-19-SARS-CoV-2
7. https://www.astralcodexten.com/p/practically-a-book-review-rootclaim
8. https://docs.google.com/document/d/1qzLC55jRfdS55oSqXJZTFItsvFsawWgNlgLxWqhCuyo/edit
9. https://michaelweissman.substack.com/p/an-inconvenient-probability-v50
10. https://blog.rootclaim.com/rootclaims-covid-19-origins-debate-results/

Write for an intelligent non-specialist. Do not assume that the centroid of observed cases is necessarily the origin. Separate what the spatial clustering itself establishes from what the full origin evidence supports.

Your report must:

- give a direct answer, with calibrated uncertainty;
- name the most important claim on which that answer depends;
- present the strongest alternative explanation and say what evidence would most change the conclusion;
- identify material dependencies among the sources where the available record establishes them—for example shared authorship, shared data, methods, citation ancestry, advisory relationships, or one source repeating another's analysis;
- avoid counting dependent sources as independent confirmation;
- say plainly what the available record does not establish;
- support material claims with citations; and
- end with a table listing the ten fixed sources, whether each was accessed and used, its evidence type, its role in the argument, and any material dependency you found. List added sources separately.

Do not discuss Epistack or compare research products. Perform the research task itself.
